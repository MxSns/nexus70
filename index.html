Accessibility

Accessibility is not merely a checklist; it's a guiding principle in every project. I design and develop with inclusivity at the forefront, making websites usable for everyone, regardless of their abilities or disabilities. From semantic HTML structures to thoughtful navigation, I ensure that your website not only meets but exceeds web accessibility standards. By prioritizing accessibility, I aim to make the digital landscape more welcoming and accommodating for all users.
